About me

About Me

 

As a Program Manager with over 14 years of experience, I bring a diverse background in technology, education, and leadership to the table. I have focused on delivering impactful programs and initiatives that drive growth and sustainability throughout my career. My specialties include program design and execution, stakeholder engagement, change management, GTM strategy development and execution, and business expansion.

Throughout my career, I have had the opportunity to work on several high-impact initiatives, including creating a talent transformation roadmap for Africa, specifically in software engineering. In this role, I have been able to leverage my expertise to build and lead strategic partnerships with governments, enterprises, and international NGOs to achieve our shared goal.

I am proud to say that I have directly trained over 4,700 teachers in-person, enabling them to improve their skills and bring the latest best practices to their students. Additionally, I have been invited to speak at several international events and conferences, where I shared my experience and insights into entrepreneurial leadership.

In addition to my professional work, I am dedicated to volunteering my time to various community organizations in different capacities. It brings me great joy to give back and contribute to my community.

Finally, I am honored to advise multiple leaders and governments in the education industry, helping to shape the future of education and ensure that every student has access to quality education.

Advisory & Strategic Partnerships

I have managed strategic partnerships with executives and cross-sector stakeholders aligned with the objectives and success metrics. I have also provided trusted advisory services to governments and engaged school leaders, policy-makers, and business decision-makers in several organizations. 

Skilling & Educational Technology Consulting

I have extensive experience in STEM education, future-ready skilling, talent transformation, and 21st-century curriculum development. He has advised governments on skilling and talent transformation roadmaps, designed program playbooks and manuals, and launched multiple skilling programs. I have also grown and managed growing communities of educators and master STEM trainers. 

Entrepreneurial Leadership

As a dedicated volunteer and speaker, I have served various community organizations in different capacities. Being a strong advocate for diversity, equity, and inclusion, I have built strong and diverse teams, which in turn  grown organizations through effective fundraising and project management. I have a solid background and experience at developing effective go-to-market strategies and facilitating customer and partner-facing demos and training sessions. 

Program Mamagement

 Over 14 years, I  have led and managed various programs and initiatives, including talent development programs, summer leadership camps, and skilling programs. I have demonstrated expertise in program design and execution, budgeting, resourcing, and scaling.
